This page is all about what it takes to be a Firefighter. If you think you have what it takes to do the job, keep reading below for more information.

Span

A career in the fire service will test you every day and can be incredibly rewarding. Firefighting requires difficult and demanding physical work. You will undergo strenuous activities and handle all types of emergencies, including life-or-death situations. The job of a firefighter is often times underestimated. A great number of candidates are overwhelmed by the long and challenging examination process necessary to find the best new recruits. Tough physical standards are necessary due to the nature of the work and the extreme level of physical fitness and mental alertness the job requires.
